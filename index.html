FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental services and procedures.

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and professional cleaning. However, depending on your oral health needs, our dentists may recommend more frequent visits.

Professional teeth whitening performed by our dentists is completely safe and does not damage your enamel. We use clinically proven whitening agents that are carefully applied to protect your teeth while achieving a brighter smile.

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. They are designed to be a permanent solution for missing teeth and integrate naturally with your jawbone for maximum durability.

For many cases, Invisalign is just as effective as traditional braces. Our orthodontist will evaluate your specific needs and recommend the best treatment option. Invisalign is particularly great for adults who want a discreet orthodontic solution.

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our front desk team will help verify your benefits and maximize your coverage. We also offer flexible payment plans for treatments not fully covered by insurance.
